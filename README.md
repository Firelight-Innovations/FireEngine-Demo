To play the game:

WASD to move around
Arrow keys to look
1 - 9 keys to switch weapons
Space to shoot

To mod the game:
Navigate to ./FireEngine Demo/_internal/Game

To add/change objects and the scene go to /Objects
To add new assets go to /Assets

Note: Not all of the paramaters in the .DAT files work.
